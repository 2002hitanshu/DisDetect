What We Offer

Types of Learning Disabilities

  • Dyslexia: Challenges with reading fluency, decoding, spelling.
  • Dyscalculia: Struggles with math, understanding numbers.
  • Dysgraphia: Issues with handwriting and written expression.
  • ADHD: Difficulty focusing, staying organized, and completing tasks.

Diagnosis Tools

Our diagnostic assessments guide you through a series of questions and tests designed to pinpoint specific disabilities. These include:

  • Detailed questionnaires.
  • Vision, hearing, and neurological screenings.
  • Reading and language assessments.

Personalized Solutions

We provide tailored strategies and tools for each learning disability:

  • Dyslexia: Structured literacy programs, text-to-speech software.
  • Dysgraphia: Occupational therapy, handwriting exercises.
  • ADHD: Digital planners, focus-enhancing tools, structured routines.

How It Works

  1. Take the Assessment: Answer a series of questions to identify potential disabilities.
  2. Get Personalized Results: Receive detailed insights into the disability and recommended solutions.
  3. Access Tools: Explore assistive technologies, strategies, and therapies tailored to your needs.

Our Research

We base our platform on trusted research and references, including:

  • ADHD: Studies from IEEE and Mayo Clinic.
  • Dyslexia: Solutions developed by industry experts.

About Us

Our platform focuses on diagnosing various learning disabilities such as Dyslexia, ADHD, Dysgraphia, and more. Our goal is to help individuals recognize their difficulties and offer personalized strategies and tools to overcome them.
